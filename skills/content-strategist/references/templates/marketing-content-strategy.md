## Instructions for AI Agents
- Strategist: Define strategic choices and tradeoffs, tie each to a measurable business outcome.
- Researcher: Validate assumptions with market, competitor, and audience evidence.
- Content Creator: Translate strategy into executable themes, assets, and narratives.
- Critic: Stress-test logic, identify weak assumptions, and flag overreach.
- Editor: Enforce clarity, remove duplication, and standardize structure.
- Validator/QuadLock: Verify KPI traceability, owner/date completeness, and decision thresholds.
